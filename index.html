About Me

During that time I also partook in a selective program called Joe Berg Seminars which facilitates various educational presentations similar to TEDtalks.

I graduated from the University of North Florida (UNF) with a BS in Computing and Information Sciences. Most of the classes I took while attending UNF were supplimented with the Java programming language with a few focusing on web design languages like HTML,Javascript, and PHP. I also took several database classes that utilized Mysql and Oracle SQL.

I worked at an Amazon shipping wharehouse during the intial COVID-19 shutdowns where I initiated the exportation of products within the facility. This position allowed me to become familiar with conveyor belt systems to the point where I could troubleshoot and resolve most belt malfunctions that occured without assistance.

Since leaving that position I have been working on improving my understanding of Python through the use of free coding boot camps. Along with strengthening my knowledge of the previously mentioned development languages and tools with various forms of online educational resources.
